Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslim of the India subcontinent.It is made with Indian spices,rice,either with meat,or eggs or vegetable such as potatoes.biryani is one of the most popular dishes in south asia,as well as among the diaspora from the region

Qorma

Korma or qorma is a dish originating in the India subcontinent,consisting of meat or vegetables braised with yogurt,water or stock,and spices to produce a thick sauce or gravy
